WHY YOU SHOULD BUY
If you prefer feeling like youre slowly melting into the bed and experiencing a deeper-cradling hug, this mattress has classic features you will enjoy.
Highlights
  • CertiPUR-US Certified
  • OEKO-TEX
  • 365-Night Trial
  • Forever Warranty

Nectar takes the cake for the best overall value mattress this year. Although theyre a relatively young company (founded in 2017), consumers are in love. Despite not reporting sales figures, Nectar is likely to be 2019s top seller. It's an all around winner.

The Nectar Mattress is made up of multiple foam layers. There are 11 inches of foam total. In addition, there is a further 1 inch of cooling foam, combined with Tencel covers. This ensures that you should have a cool sleeping experience. The Nectar Mattress provides good overall support for all sleep positions, and especially excels for side sleepers. Contouring and pressure relief is excellent.

Nectars flagship product has a medium firmness (6.5/10), which is the most common preference. Nectars medium firmness is perfect for all common sleeping positions. There is very little motion transfer and considering this is an all-foam product, the edge support is fantastic.

Why is it special? ctars mattress provides a very unique sleeping experience. While its a memory foam product, the overall feel of the mattress is different to typical memory foam products. Despite this, with a Nectar, youll feel well-supported and cozy, just like any other memory foam product.

If youre not convinced, Nectar offers the best return policy in the industry. They have a risk free 365 night return policy with a lifetime warranty.

 

Layla (True Memory Foam Feel)

WHY YOU SHOULD BUY
Layla is double-sided, with Soft and Firm sides. You can discover the type of support and pressure relief that suits your body best.
Highlights
  • Double-Sided
  • CertiPUR-US Certified
  • 120-Night Trial
  • Lifetime Guarantee
  • Made in the USA
Click Here for Price

Despite getting off of the ground in 2017, Layla has proved themselves to be one of the most innovative memory foam based beds. As their foam is copped-infused, and covered with ThermaGel, Layla has some of the best cooling properties on the market. Layla beds are smart in the sense that the cooling only activates when heat or moisture are detected, so it wont keep you cool during winter nights. Some studies indicate that copper has been known to aid inflammatory diseases (rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis), and improve blood flow.

Layla has four layers. Its 10 inches thick, made up of 3 inches of copper-infused memory foam on the Soft side, 2 inches of convoluted support foam, 4.5 inches of the base support foam and 1 inch of copper-infused memory foam for the firm side. Of all of the bed-in-a-box companies, Layla was the first to offer a lifetime guarantee on their beds.

Many people aren't sure if they prefer a firm or soft feel. Layla has thought of this, and allows you to flip the mattress over for a completely different feel. With their build quality, comfort and cooling properties, Layla has solidified itself as one of the top mattresses of 2019.

DreamCloud makes this list because it offers a truly high end sleeping experience for a very reasonable price. DreamClouds materials are high end, providing extremely high value for the price. In addition to their customer satisfaction ratings and quality construction, DreamCloud offers a year long, risk free trial. This means that you can try out a DreamCloud mattress for a year to ensure it suits your sleeping style. DreamCloud is a hybrid mattress and it is built to impress. Its 15 inches high and has eight layers of premium coils and foam.

As its a Euro style mattress, the bed begins with a tufted cashmere cover. Most mattress companies with tufted covers only sew together the initial layers, but DreamCloud attaches all of its eight layers together. This means that a DreamCloud mattress is engineered to be more durable than other mattresses that primarily use glues. In the DreamCloud, latex and memory foam is blended together to create a firm support system. There are five support zones, made up of pocketed coils and high density memory foam.

DreamCloud has low overheads by only selling online, and using minimal (but robust) packaging. This allows them to pass the savings onto their customers. DreamCloud customers are able to purchase big brand/hotel quality mattresses at a fraction of the retail pricing.

If youre in the market for a natural latex mattress, then you should take a look at this bed. Zenhaven is manufactured by an Saatva, a well respected player in the mattress industry.

The Zenhaven is ten inches thick and flippable. Making up the mattress is 1.5 inches of Talalay latex. This provides the mattress with comfort and bounce. The following two layers are three inches each, and depending on which way the mattress is flipped, are either a support or transitional layer. The final layer is another 1.5 inches of Talalay latex, which when flipped, makes the mattress rather firm.

The soft side of the Zenhaven is a 5/10 in regards to firmness. The firm side is a 7/10. Most sleepers will prefer the firm side of the Zenhaven.

Why choose this bed? If youre not a fan of memory foam, but you like a bed with fantastic cooling, bounce and support, then the Zenhaven is a great option. Despite being slightly more pricey than some of its competitors, it has terrific consumer ratings.

With more bounce and airflow than other online brands, you will enjoy a traditional feel that is enhanced with lumbar support.

  • 15-Year Warranty

Instead of making a move into the foam mattress market, Saatva took a different route. People are hesitant to consider innerspring mattresses when compared with foam mattresses, as foam mattresses offer fantastic contouring and support. However, Saatva combines the best properties from foam and innerspring beds, resulting in their pillow-top hybrid. Excellent cooling and edge support is provided by a coil-on-coil system, while memory foam and a European-style pillow top provide unmatched comfort.

Saatva offers three different firmness levels (plush, medium and firm), and two variations of thickness (11.5 inches and 14.5 inches). A thicker option doesnt really provide any difference in the way the mattress sleeps, its mostly an aesthetic change. The shorter version is compatible with adjustable bases.

All things considered, Saatva is one of the best beds you can buy. If youre looking for a luxury mattress with excellent cooling, support and durability, Saatva is a fantastic choice.

Its important to note that the Nolah team support animal conservation. When you purchase a Nolah mattress, you get to choose a threatened animal to help protect. Nolah donates 7% of their profits to wildlife protection each year. Theyre a great company supporting a great cause, and theyre very socially conscious.

The Nolah founders have 20 years of experience in the mattress industry. Their aim was to switch up the industry by providing a new feel. This lead them to creating Nolahs proprietary Nolah AirFoam. This foam helps to reduce pressure in the hip, shoulder and back regions.

Nolah is a 10 inch mattress, with three foam layers. Up top we have 2 inches of Nolahs AirFoam. This is fantastic for side sleepers. Following this, we have a single inch of transitional foam, which has very similar properties to latex. The base layer is 7 inches thick, and is made up of mega-density polyurethane, designed for continuous support.

What makes it special? Nolah is advertised as havingfour times less peak pressure than similar memory foam products. If youre a side sleeper that has too much pressure, the Nolah is for you.

Nolah offers a 15 year warranty and a comparable 120-night-trial. Theres nothing to lose by trying them out.

  • 100-Night Trial

Out of all of the mattress brands, Helix has the most exciting manufacturing method. Youre able to fully customize the firmness, cooling, feel and elasticity of your mattress by completing a short questionnaire on their site. Put simply, Helix is a hybrid, made from coils and polyfoam. The springs add a great cooling effect, and the foam offers more pressure relief and contouring.

Helix offers more than nine different feels, with each feel having different names and colors. By filling out their quiz, youll know which feel is right for you. Sleeping with a partner? Youre able to choose a split solution. Each side of the Helix can have a different feel.

If youre worried about sleeping hot, but your partner is worried about sleeping cold, or you dont know which type of feel youre after, Helix offers a great way to select a custom made mattress, according to your needs. This is done at a very competitive price. We recommend.
